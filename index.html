Solvable - YSWS

Design a solution to a real problem and get it 3D printed and shipped right to you!

Start Your Project

What is this?

Solvable is a YSWS (You Ship, We Ship) programe. Find a problem or inconvenience in yours or someone else's life and design a solution in CAD and get it 3D printed.

How it Works

1

Find a Problem

Identify an inconvenience or problem in your daily life that needs solving.

2

Design & Model

Create a solution and model it up in any CAD programe

3

Submit your Project

Submit and wait for your project to be reviewed

4

Recieve and Build

Recieve your grant and get it printed, or it wil lcome straight from HQ or printer legion!

Requirements

What Qualifies?

Your project should:

  • Solve a real, specific problem
  • Be something you can physiclly build
  • Be your own original work
  • Fit within a standard 3D printer bed - 256mmx256mm
  • Not have been submitted to any other Hack Club programe

Build & Documentation Expectations

  • Explain the problem and how you solved it
  • Have photos of the build process in your Github repo
  • Have any and all related files in your github repo
  • Have a clear Journal
    • Your research and CAD progress documented with photos
    • Describing how you brainstormed your solution

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to use hardware at all?
Nope! It can just be 3D printed, as long as it solves the problem.
What is the max size?
While there is not a strict restriction, it should be able to fit on most 3D printers (256x256mm).
Can I work with a partner?
Yes, as long as only one person submits the projects.
Can I design a solution for a friend?
Yes, but you should encourage you friend to design it themself. You can still help though! Don't give them the fish, teach them to catch!
What is the max number of submissions one can do?
Probably 3. Stay tuned as this may change! Updates in Solvable Slack Channel!
